Hello and Welcome

Here, you can find out more about LA, who we are, and what we do.

Although chances are, if you are already here, then you probably have a decent idea of what that is.

Pops, Bangs, and Burning Rubber...

We are a collective of modified car enthusiasts. Our vision is to create an established community of like-minded enthusiasts in the local area, with aims to expand further afield within the UK and eventually abroad.

LA aims to provide a range of experiences, from chilled-out underground meets, to huge, bursting shows, mental track days, drift events, and everything in between. We draw inspiration from the North American car scene.
