Welcome to Apex Automotive

We are an established automotive tuning and modification specialist based in West Yorkshire, providing a comprehensive end-to-end service to meet all of your customisation and modification needs. What sets us apart from our competitors is that we don't just take a list of jobs, tick them off, and send you on your way. At Apex Automotive, you are guaranteed a high quality service, and you play an integral part in the whole process, working together with our skilled team to ensure only the best outcome.

Starting with an initial consultation, we set up a face-to-face meeting where we can really get to know each other, and where you tell us exactly what is required. Following this, a project plan is developed, which is then followed through to completion, meeting your time expectations where possible. At all stages, communication is open and transparent, and we even encourage our customers to pop in and see how their project is progressing. The end goal is a finished product which we guarantee you will be satisfied with.
